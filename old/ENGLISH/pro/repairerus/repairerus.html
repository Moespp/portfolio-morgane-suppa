REPAIRER

#FirstPerson #Platformer #3D #Si-fi

Repairer is a frenetic Platformer 3D from Bordeaux studio HammerHead, set in a tranquil digital universe where we play as FIVE, a maintenance robot from the Green Agency sent to repair anomalies through simulations. I worked on the Level Design. for this project.

Engine : Unreal Engine 4.27

Tools: Miro, Trello, Discord

In this simulation, FIVE navigates through various environments, jumping, sliding (on the ground or on walls), using a grappling hook, and running from one platform to another. This game focuses on exploration and contemplation. The levels are designed with an open structure, allowing players to go anywhere and choose their own path to reach the main objective of each level. As players traverse these diverse environments, they can enjoy complete freedom of movement in a relaxing atmosphere, while assisting various robots in a non-violent gaming experience.
