Morgane SUPPA

I am

Since my earliest days, I've always been drawn to art and video games. Level Design and Narration were immediately the specialties in which I felt most at ease, followed by Environmental Storytelling and Level Art. My artistic profile merges with my technical skills. My artistic skills are the foundation of my collaborations with the other teams, an alliance that I believe is essential to bring projects to life. I'm lucky enough to love my job, and it's a pleasure for me to share with you what I've achieved.

I began my career with an Escape Game (Secret Door), where I worked on 4 competitive escape rooms (a first in France) and I was Game Master. I continued my career with the independent Toulouse studio Dreamirl and the Bordeaux studio HammerHead.

Alongside my career in video games, I am the author and illustrator of a comic entitled A Crow's Nest, an adventure in a Dark Fantasy world.

I also draw for role-playing campaigns and create my own campaigns within universes where I've created the full World Building.

Each step has shaped my passion and honed my skills.

A few points about me:

- Graduate in Game Design and Gamification (2020-2023)

- Fable The Lost Chapter, Titanfall 2, Ori and The Will Of The Wisps, Apex Legends, Baldur's Gate 3 are my favorite games.

- Self-taught Artist for over 15 years, I do realism as well as very dark semi-realism.

- Pianist for over 10 years, I also play guitar, ukulele and i sing.

- Creator of many Role Play universes on Discord but also Game Master.
