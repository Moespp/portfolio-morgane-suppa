ECHOES OF THE RESTLESS

#Platformer #Cartoon #3D #fantasy #FinalStudyProject

Echoes of the restless is a 3D platformer where you take on the role of an entity returned from the dead, who must use his ingenuity to travel between worlds in a most curious way. in the most curious of ways: how to die.

For this project I worked on Game & Level Design, le Narrative Design and Level Art.

Gameplay

The disembodiment mechanic is crucial for progression. Here, the player encounters a frightened creature hiding in a bush. To progress, the player must wait for the creature to come out of hiding and then jump on it. This action unlocks a previously inaccessible passage in the other world. This mechanic encourages reflection and environmental observation.

The game is punctuated by discoveries of narrative locations. These moments allow Cethlenn to tell the story of the places and deepen the context of their shared quest. Players learn more about the world's history. These narrative moments add depth to exploration, offering a welcome pause while enriching the game experience.
At the end of each level, players must use their mastery of skills acquired during final confrontations. Here, in a temple infested with snakes, the player must avoid their field of vision to prevent returning to the last checkpoint. Disembodiment is necessary for progression, but patrolling snakes pose a major challenge. Players must be quick to evade the snakes' sight and move forward.

Each level is divided into three parts: The game offers progression through three phases: Tutorial, where the player familiarizes themselves with the basic mechanics; Practice, allowing the player to refine their newly acquired skills; and Mastery, where the player is tested on everything they have learned throughout the level. The main mechanic involves finding a way to disembodied to switch worlds and uncover passages for progression. The narrative adds a storytelling layer, enhancing player engagement through themes of redemption.
